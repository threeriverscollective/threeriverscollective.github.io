About Three Rivers Collective

Three Rivers Collective is an ecommerce holding company based in Wisconsin, United States, specializing in sporting goods and outdoor products. We manufacture and sell apparel, hard goods, and sporting equipment directly to consumers and via B2B channels. Our production facilities are primarily located in Thailand, Taiwan, China, and Japan, ensuring high-quality output through efficient global supply chains. Our team includes full-time, part-time, and contract professionals from the United States, Southeast Asia, North America, and South America, combining dedicated U.S. leadership with a diverse international network to support operations and innovation.
